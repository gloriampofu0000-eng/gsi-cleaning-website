About Us

GSI Cleaning Services is based in Rhodesfield, Kempton Park. We deliver professional cleaning solutions — residential, commercial and industrial — with a focus on quality, safety and customer satisfaction.

We provide deep cleaning, fumigation, end-of-tenancy cleaning, post-construction clean-up, yard cleanup and upholstery care. Our teams are trained, insured and use safe cleaning methods and equipment.
